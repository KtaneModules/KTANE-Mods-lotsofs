On the Subject of Kim’s Game

Look on them as long as thou wilt, stranger. Count. One look is enough for me. When thou hast counted and handled and art sure that thou canst remember them all, I cover them, and thou must tell over the tally to me, the bomb. I will do the same.

  • This module presents a belt, onto which are displayed various icons.
  • Remember all the icons.
  • Whenever you're ready, spin the knob attached to the top-right of the belt.
  • This will spin the belt, in turn moving all the icons out of view.
  • After a while, the icons will come spinning back in different positions, along with several others.
  • Press every icon that was present previously to disarm the module.
