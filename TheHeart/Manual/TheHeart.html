Keep Talking and Nobody Explodes Mod The Heart

On the Subject of The Heart

It's true; bombs can feel emotions too. How exciting is that?

  • This module presents a beating heart. It pumps blood to the other modules on the bomb.
  • If the heart beats normally, no action needs to be taken.
  • Incurring a strike on the bomb will cause the heart to stop beating. Additionally, the heart will stop beating exactly a minute after they've been started, as counted by the bomb's countdown timer. Lastly, the heart will also stop beating when the bomb's countdown timer reaches 1 second remaining.
  • A stopped heart must be defribrilated. This can be done by simply pressing the heart once.
  • Failure to defibrilate a stopped heart in time will incur a strike on the bomb.
  • Do not attempt to defibrilate a beating heart. This will incur a strike on the bomb.
  • The module can be solved by solving other modules. Solved modules do not require blood. Therefore, when enough modules are solved the heart is no longer needed.
  • This point occurs when the amount of times the heart has been defibrilated is greater than or equal to the amount of unsolved modules on the bomb plus two times the amount of solved other hearts.
  • The module will then automatically solve the next time the heart stops beating. Solved hearts will no longer require defibrilation.
